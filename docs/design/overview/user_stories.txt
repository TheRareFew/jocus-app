USER STORIES

Creator:
    - Receives suggested meme formats.
    - Edits video with features tailored for memes
    - Is given insights on how to "go viral" (this is a win-win for the creator and the app company)
    - Is given audience insights such as common interests among audience members


User:
    - Receives scrolling feed of memes
        - keep simple, we're really just trying to focus on the creator experience here
    

Universal:
    - New users have an onboarding flow
        - Are you a creator?
        - What type of memes do you want to make/see?
            - animals, pop culture, politics, etc.
